WHAT IS BIBLE TOUR ADVENTURE?

Bible Tour Adventure (BTA) is a non-denominational Christian discipleship platform created to help believers grow in consistency, understanding, and obedience to God’s Word. Through a structured and accountable process, participants are guided to read, study, and engage with the complete Bible, developing spiritual discipline and a genuine love for Scripture.

  • A guided journey through the 66 books of the Bible

  • Partnership with the Holy Spirit in learning and application

  • Structured accountability and spiritual discipline

  • Two discipleship phases: 8 months and 12 months

  • Open to believers across denominations, locations, and nations

How the Platform Works

Bible Tour Adventure operates through a clearly defined, structured discipleship process designed to build consistency, discipline, and spiritual maturity.

  • Phase One — Foundation (8 Months)

  • Designed for first-time participants
  • Focuses on consistency, discipline, and foundational engagement with God’s Word
  • Four intakes annually

  • Phase Two — Growth & Maturity (12 Months)

  • Designed for returning participants
  • Emphasizes deeper accountability, spiritual maturity, and sustained discipline
  • Two intakes annually

  • The Result

Participants grow in:

  • Spiritual discipline
  • Consistent Bible engagement
  • Deeper understanding of God’s Word
  • Love for truth and obedience to Scripture

THE BURDEN BEHIND THE VISION

Bible Tour Adventure was not born from convenience, trends, or popularity, but from a burden to see believers grounded, disciplined, and established in God’s Word. What began as a divine instruction has grown into a structured platform where believers are guided beyond inspiration into consistency, accountability, and obedience to Scripture. This vision is rooted in unity beyond geography, denomination, or background, anchored solely in God’s Word. Read the full vision and journey on the About BTA page.
